Superman

It can be said that Superman is one of the most famous superhero characters in comic books. His real name is Clark Kent, and he works for Daily Planet as a journalist. He is one of the nonhuman members in the league. He was born as Kal-El on another planet called Krypton, so he is a Kryptonian.

When Superman was a baby, there was a chaos in Krypton. His parents had to send him in a rocket to the Earth because their planet was about to explode. After Superman left Krpton, his parents died there as the others. He was found by Jonathan and Martha Kent when he reached the earth, and they raised him as their son in the town of Smallville. He faced a lot of problems during childhood because of not being a human. Thus, his new parents, Jonathan and Martha, have also assisted to adapt to Earth conditions. He has lived a normal life until he is a young man, then he decided to use his powers for humanity. In addition, he takes as a goal to be hope for them.

"It's not an 'S'. On my world, it means hope."

Lois Lane is an significant character for Superman. She is portrayed as his colleague, competitor, love interest and wife in various comic books. In Daily Planet, he has some friends, Jimmy Olsen and Perry White. They know what he does secretly, and totally supports and helps him.
His best-known enemy is Lex Luthor, and he is obsessed with destroying Superman. He tries everything to accomplish that. Hence, he has also faced the league many times. Another enemy of his is Doomsday. He is as strong as Superman, and is the first villian to kill Superman in physical combat.
